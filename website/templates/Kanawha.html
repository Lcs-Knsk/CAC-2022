Kanawha State Forest claims 9,300 acres and is located just south of the State's capital. This close proximity to Charleston has made it a haven for recreational activities. The Forest offers picnic locations, hiking, mountain biking, and skiing opportunities. The park also has a swimming pool and playground, as well as a campground.

Hunting and fishing are available to licensed individuals in certain areas of the forest. Fishing areas include: Kanawha River, Ridenour Park, Coonskin Park, and Wine Cellar Park.
